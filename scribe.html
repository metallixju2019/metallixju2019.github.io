Poster Presentation (Scribe)

They say a picture is worth a thousand words, even we believe it, and hence we bring to you a chance to showcase your knowledge through images. Metallix’19 presents before you SCRIBE, the poster presentation competition. We make learning all the more fun, pulling out the theories from classroom and giving you a chance to pour all that you have learnt in posters, and what more.. There are exciting prizes to be won. So what's the wait, register now.


Rules and Regulations :

1. Team Size : Maximum 2 participants.
2. Participants will be required to submit an abstract of maximum 2 pages on their original scientific research or review articles based on the topic : LATEST TRENDS IN METALLURGICAL AND MATERIAL SCIENCE.
3. The abstract should be submitted latest by 25th February 2019.
4. The team will get 8 to 10 minutes to present their papers.
5. Abstracts are to be mailed at metallixju2019@gmail.com.
6. The decision of the judges will be final.
