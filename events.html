Know More

Metallix 2019 is a two-day long techno-metal spectacle, involving students of Metallurgical and Material Engineering from different colleges and universities across India; where various technical events will be held. The event is aimed at creating a congenial platform where students can interact with stalwarts in this discipline, engaged in research as well as industry.
Sustainable development is an integral part of economic development in all countries. The balance between the need of humanity to produce and the desire to not destroy the planet in the process is constantly questioned and shaken. Concept of Industry 4.0 includes sustainability as an integral part and it directly affects production processes. Manufacturing intelligence, facilitated by smart data integration will impact value chains, creating sustainability benefits. This year, we chose to reflect upon this and hence the topic of the National Symposium is Sustainable Development of the Iron and Steel Industry and implementation of Industry 4.0 where stalwarts from these sectors will speak on the various methods being implemented currently as well as the technologies that are under the course of research and development.
The National Student Metallurgists' Meet is aimed at a meaningful interaction among students and professionals. The symposium is bound to have a lasting effect on the young minds..
